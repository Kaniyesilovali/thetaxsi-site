<h2>Why a fixed price beats a metered taxi</h2>
<p>The taxi rank outside arrivals looks convenient. In practice three things happen: you negotiate a price before the wheels turn, a surcharge appears if it's late, and if you've landed at Larnaca or Paphos you may be asked to change vehicles at the border — moving your cases from one car to another in the buffer zone, to put it politely. A fixed price airport transfer removes all three: you see the figure when you book, the same car takes you door to door, and neither traffic nor the hour changes it. For practical notes on cash and tipping, see our <a href="/en/blog/north-cyprus-money-tipping-guide/">money and tipping guide</a>.</p>
